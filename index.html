Rights of the disabled
Give the disabled what they deserve, they are humans too! We are here to discuss the rights that should be given to those with disabilities.

The disabled need more than one choice!

People who are disabled are heavily impacted by COVID-19, as their healthcare providers only cover certain processes. They do not have enough money to pay for other services, leaving them to endure the main and suffering.

Image taken from U.S. Department of Defense.

More than 23 million adults under the age of 65 have a disability.

Image taken from NYT.

The mistreatment is seen worldwide.

Those who are disabled are not given the same treatment as those who do not have a disability. They are often looked down upon and denied simple things like education and jobs. Although many have gathered together to request equal treatment, they were denied and kicked out of offices.

Image taken from Boston Herald.

Our Message

The importance of giving people simple rights should be widely heard. No one should be treated any differently because of disabilities holding them back. Those who are disabled should be able to live like any other human being. It is not something that should be looked down upon nor is it something unheard of. We need to educate the public on how common it is to have/obtain a disability.
